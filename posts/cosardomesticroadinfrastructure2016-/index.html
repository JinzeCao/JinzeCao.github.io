  • Results

    1. “First, the cost of an average shipment over a high capacity expressway is about 70% lower than it is over single-lane roads.” (Coşar 和 Demir, 2016, p. 232)
    2. “Second, the present value of a 10-year stream of trade flows generated by a one-dollar investment in road infrastructure ranges between $0.7 and $2.” (Coşar 和 Demir, 2016, p. 232)
    3. “the reduction in transportation costs is greater the more transportation-sensitive an industry is.” (Coşar 和 Demir, 2016, p. 232)
